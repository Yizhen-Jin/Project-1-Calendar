About the App

The purpose of this app is to provide a digital planner to organize your daily tasks. You can insert data into a to-do list and a schedule and get them organized by color code. With this app, you will be prepared for the tasks you need to finish as well as productive for the daily commitments. Having a digital planner also benefits in saving money, reuseable, less waste, and also easy to access.

Tired of using paper planner? Let's start with our digital planner!
